Mini FAQ

How to power on / power off?
Software: go to http://10.100.3.132 and press the top left button.
Hardware: the controller is a small box at the lower left corner of the LED arch - it has a power cable going to the mains. If you disconnect it, the controller + the LEDs will stop.
