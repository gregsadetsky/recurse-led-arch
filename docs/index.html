Mini FAQ

How to power on / power off?
Software: go to http://10.100.3.132 and press the top left button.
Hardware: the controller is a small box at the lower left corner of the LED arch - it's connected to a bigger metal box (the power supply) which has a black power cable going to the mains. If you disconnect that cable, the controller + the LEDs will stop. TLDR: pull the labelled plug from the wall.
What's controlling what?
The 24V power supply is going into a QuinLED An-Penta-Plus, an ESP32-based device that is running WLED and has an ethernet port, which is connected to the hub's ethernet/internet. The LED strip is a "High Density COB Color Chasing Lights Addressable RGB LED Strip", made out of the classic individually-addressable WS2811 rgb pixels.
How many pixels is that?
There are 192 addressable "pixels" (each "pixel" is made out of 3 rgb dots, as far as I remember). I forgot how many are on the left, top, and right side - but I think they're symmetric-al-ish enough. Ping me to let me know the exact number if you end up measuring it! Cheers
